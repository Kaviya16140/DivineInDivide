Hi there… welcome in :)

If you’re from where I’m from, this might feel a little familiar. Maybe a little too familiar.

“Divide in Divine” is an interactive web experience I made to play with ideas of hierarchy and caste—but not in a heavy, lecture-y way. I’m not here to tell you what to think. I just want you to try something out. As you move through the site, you’ll see familiar figures like Krishna, Vishnu, and Shiva. You can browse them, compare them, and even “weigh” them against each other.

It starts off pretty simple—maybe even a bit fun. Click around, make choices, see what happens. But stay with it. At some point, it might start to feel a little… weird. That’s kind of the point.

I’m not expecting you to actually decide which god is “better” or “more important.” It’s more about noticing what it feels like to place anyone into a system like this. If you pause, hesitate, or feel slightly uncomfortable making those choices, you’re right where I want you to be.

Go on, try it out.
