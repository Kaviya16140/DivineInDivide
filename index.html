As you move through this space, take part in unfamiliarity. Feel the discomfort and when you do, pause for a moment and draw the connection. Is this something new or does it echo something you already know?

I’ve seen a lot... who gets remembered, who gets ignored, and how even the divine has its own quiet pecking order. Things up here aren’t as perfect or balanced as they might seem. So I brought you here. This isn’t just a place to look at myths, it’s a place to play with them. To compare gods, to weigh them, to see what happens when you start putting value on something that’s usually unquestioned.

I am Panjurli Daiva. A spirit deity. I wander where forests grow thick and stories don’t quite belong to one world or another. People call on me in rituals, but I’m just as comfortable watching from the sidelines, moving between gods and humans, picking up things others miss.
